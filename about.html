About

Connor Solawetz was born and raised in Evergreen, Colorado. Growing up in the mountains, he cultivated a love of adventure and the outdoors. Many days were spent roaming around the mountains near his home as well as playing sports such as football, lacrosse, rugby, and hockey throughout high school. After graduating from Evergreen High School in 2015, Connor spent a brief semester at Montana State University in Bozeman before moving back home. The next several years were spent working in a restaurant while attaining an Associate's of Science Degree from Red Rocks Community College in Denver. Connor currently is based out of Lakewood, Colorado.


Currently working in an Alaskan-themed restaurant and bar, Connor has cultivated excellent interpersonal skills over 10 years in the customer service industry. Always ready for a new challenge, he is currently working through a Coding Bootcamp. Some of Connor's interests include music, reading, writing, and adventuring outdoors.
